About us

In a world where everyone is trying to hold your hand,
we believe that letting go and taking charge of your own life results in greater self-improvement,
because only you are responsible for your life.

We value things like being audacious, to grow together and be a better you, staying determined to your goal and staying authentic to yourself throughout the process.

Our chosen logo represents us and our subjects in all its aspects, a lone wolf combined with the ever changing moon.
We invite the subject to join us and become part of a pack.
The subject will go through a transformation of a better self like the moon.
