Design Justification

Our Brand focusses on being adventurous and taking control of your own life. Embracing the values of being audacious, growth, determination and authenticity. We found that there are a lot of people out there that are looking for their sense of self and we want to offer them inspiring trips or workshops to give the guidance in taking control over their own life and personal growth. Because we focus on personal growth we want our customer to be the one that shines. Which is why we chose for a black and white color scheme and a simple and minimalistic layout.
